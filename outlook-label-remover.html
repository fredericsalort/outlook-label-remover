Privacy Policy

Outlook External Label Remover is a Chrome extension that helps clean up your Outlook inbox by removing the “External” label from emails in the Outlook web interface.

This extension:

  • Does not collect or store any personal or sensitive data
  • Does not communicate with any servers or external services
  • Only modifies the display of content locally within your browser

Your privacy is fully respected. This extension runs entirely on your device and never accesses or shares any of your information.
